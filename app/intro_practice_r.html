Reading practice

Let's read some casual sentences and repeat after me.

For the learners who are not used to reading yet, don't try to understand the meaning of every sentence.
Just focus on listening and repeating to become familiar with the Korean accent and pronunciation.

For the learners who are intermediate level or above, try to pronounce them like a native, and figure out what are the differences when we read naturally.
If you have learned Korean by yourself with a book, you might find it difficult to understand real conversations even though you already know all of the words and grammar in the sentences.

That's why you should practice listen and repeat a lot.
