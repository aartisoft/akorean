Vowels

Korean has a total 21 vowels. But it's easy because half of them are combined vowels. So the main vowels are only 10!

Different from consonants, every vowel uses the throat. So it's easier to make the sound compared to consonants.

I'll give you a tip. While you practice reading vowels, it would be helpful to think about the mouth shape and size.
