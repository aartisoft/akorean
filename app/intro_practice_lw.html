Awesome Korean

Listening and Writing practice

There are 3 levels in this practice.

Level 1 : Hangul without Batchim

Level 2 : Hangul with Batchim

Level 3 : Hangul with basic words

There are 10 problems in each time. You can make correct the Hangul after listening to the audio.
Level 1 & 2, you can choose the correct answer from the random consonants and vowels.
level 3, you should write the correct words by yourself.

Don't worry if you didn't get a good score. It could be really difficult because it's the only one character(L1 & L2) and we can't see speaker's mouth shape. The only important thing is to keep practicing.
