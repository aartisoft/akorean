Learning tip

When you try to memorize verbs and adjectives, it's better to make your real sentences.

For example,
"I have a Thai friend."
"I'll stay in Thailand for 4 years."
...
I made these sentences in Thai when I first started learning Thai. And it helps me to remember the words and grammar easily. It also gives me a chance to practice speaking about my real life. {{{footer}}}
