Learning tip

When you try to memorize verb/adjective, it's better to make your real sentences.

For example,
"I have a Thai friend."
"I'll stay in Thailand for 4 years."
...
When I learn Thai, I made these sentences in Thai with my real story. It makes me to remember the word and grammar easily and also I could have a chance to speak a lot in Thailand. {{{footer}}}
