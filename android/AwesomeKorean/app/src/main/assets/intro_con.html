Awesome Korean

Korean has 14 consonants and 5 double consonants only.

We can make a sound of each consonants by using articulators like lips, tongue, mouth, throat and nose.
That's why we should think about correct position of each articulators.
That will makes you to pronounce Korean like native Korean.

Some of cosonants would be similar with your language.
But some of others would be difficult to pronounce exactly.
Because you have never made that sound in your life.
So your muscles in your mouth are not strong enough to make that new sound.

Fortunately, we can train our muscles with practice. And it won't take much time.

Let's start!
